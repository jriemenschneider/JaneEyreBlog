Book Recommendations

Here I share my top 3 books every month of the year! All can be find on good reads! Xoxo Plain Jane

Sort: Jan Feb March April May June July Aug Sept Oct Nov Dec

January

  1. The Jane Austen Society by Natalie Jenner
  2. Dear Edward by Ann Napolitano
  3. Mexican Gothic by Silvia Moreno-Garcia

Febuary

  1. Take a Hint, Dani Brown by Talia Hibbert
  2. If It Bleeds by Stephen King
  3. The New Wilderness by Diane Cook

March

  1. You Should See Me in a Crown by Leah Johnson
  2. Child of Virtue and Vengeance by Tomi Adeyemi
  3. Midnight Sun by Stephanie Meyer

April

  1. On the Horizon by Lois Lowry
  2. A Deadly Education by Naomi Novik
  3. When No One is Watching by Alyssa Cole

May

  1. The Inheritance Games by Jennifer Lynn Barnes
  2. Long Bright River by Liz Moore
  3. My Dark Vanessa by Kate Elizabeth Russell

June

  1. A Black Women's History of the United States by Diana Ramey Berry, Kali Nicole Gross
  2. Stamped from the Beginning: The Definitive History of Racist Ideas in America by Ibram X. Kendi
  3. How to Be an Antiracist by Ibram X. Kendi
Note: This month's recs are from some very special black authors about Black History for BLM! I would encourage you all to read and learn from them, so we can change the future and finally do what is right and necessary.

July

  1. The Girl With the Louding Voice by Abi Daré
  2. The Searcher by Tana French
  3. The Vanishing Half by Brit Bennett

August

  1. The Guest List by Lucy Foley
  2. Network Effect by Marsha Wells
  3. The Space Between Worlds by Micaiah Johnson

September

  1. The End Of October by Lawrence Wright
  2. The Pull of the Stars by Emma Donoghue
  3. The Midnight Library by Matt Haig

October

  1. The Glass Hotel by Emily St. John Mandel
  2. The Hollow Places by T. Kingfisher
  3. Tender Is the Flesh by Agustina Bazterrica, Sarah Moses (Translator), Агустина Бастеррика

November

  1. The Book of Lost Friends by Lisa Wingate
  2. The Year of the Witching by Alexis Henderson
  3. Transcendant Kingdom by Yaa Gyasi

December

  1. Axiom's End by Lindsay Ellis
  2. Home Before Dark by Riley Sager
  3. Jane Eyre by Charlotte Brontë, Michael Mason (Editor)
